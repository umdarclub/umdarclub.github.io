About Us

UMD AR Club is an organization in which great people change reality. This is a place where Augmented and Mixed Reality pioneers and developers get together to build fantastic applications, while using the Hololens and other hollow devices. Our members mainly work in teams, but individuals are welcomed as well. So, feel free to come out and enjoy what UMD AR has to offer!
